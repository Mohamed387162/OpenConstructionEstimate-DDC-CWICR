## 2. Permitted uses (nominative fair use)

You may use the DDC Marks without prior written permission only
for factual, descriptive reference - for example:

- "data sourced from DDC CWICR"
- "uses OpenConstructionEstimate"
- academic citation of the dataset
  (see [CITATION.cff](./CITATION.cff) and
  [NOTICE](./NOTICE) for the preferred format).

Such use must:

- **not alter, abbreviate, translate, or stylise** the marks
  beyond Licensor's published logo-usage guidelines;
- **not suggest** sponsorship, endorsement, affiliation, or
  partnership with Licensor where none exists;
- include a disclaimer where your product or service is not
  produced by Licensor: *"This product is not affiliated with,
  endorsed by, or sponsored by DataDrivenConstruction."*

## 3. CC BY 4.0 attribution ≠ trademark licence

The Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International licence that
covers the Data (see [LICENSE](./LICENSE) and
[LICENSE-DATA.txt](./LICENSE-DATA.txt)) requires you to **give
attribution** to Licensor when you redistribute or build upon
the Data. That attribution requirement is satisfied by the
factual nominative use described in Section 2 above.

However, CC BY 4.0 **does not grant trademark rights**
(clause 2(b)(2) of the licence expressly reserves them to the
licensor). You therefore must not use the DDC Marks to
distinguish your product or service beyond the factual
"data sourced from DDC CWICR" and similar references.

Without prior written permission from Licensor you may NOT:

(a) use the DDC Marks in a product name, company name, or

    `openconstructionestimate-cloud`, `ddc-data.net`);
(b) reproduce, modify, or stylise DDC logos or wordmarks;
(c) sell, licence, or monetise derivative data products under
    a name that is confusingly similar to a DDC Mark;
(d) register DDC Marks as trademarks, service marks, domain
    names, or social-media handles in any jurisdiction;
(e) use DDC Marks to imply that your derivative dataset is
    authoritative, complete, or endorsed by Licensor when it
    is not;
(f) strip attribution from the Data and replace it with your
    own branding as if you had compiled the Data yourself.

Use that crosses the line between permitted nominative fair use
and prohibited use is a trademark infringement under
**§§ 14-15 MarkenG** (Germany), **15 U.S.C. §§ 1114, 1125**
(United States), and **EU Regulation 2017/1001 Art. 9** (EU).

## 6. Forks and derivative datasets

Derivatives of the Data redistributed under CC BY 4.0 must:

- provide the required attribution in the format shown in
  [LICENSE](./LICENSE) Section 1;
- **change the product name** if the derivative is distributed
  or marketed as a separate product or service (do not publish
  a derivative dataset as "CWICR-\<anything\>" or
  "OpenConstructionEstimate-\<anything\>" on data platforms);
- indicate clearly what has been changed from the original;
- not imply endorsement by Licensor.
