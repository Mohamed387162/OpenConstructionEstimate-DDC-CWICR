# Patent Non-Assertion Policy

**Version 1.0 — Effective April 2026**

Artem Boiko / DataDrivenConstruction.io ("Licensor") makes the
following unilateral patent non-assertion commitment in respect
of this repository.

## 1. Definitions

- **"Software"** means the adapter scripts (Python, JavaScript,
  R, Rust, shell), n8n workflows, AI agent instructions,
  Dockerfiles, and build automation distributed under the
  Apache License, Version 2.0 in this repository.
- **"Data"** means the dataset files (CSV, XLSX, Parquet,
  Qdrant snapshots) distributed under the Creative Commons
  Attribution 4.0 International licence in this repository.
- **"Patent Claim"** means any claim of a patent now owned,
  controlled, or licensable by Licensor anywhere in the world.

## 2. Non-Assertion Commitment

Licensor covenants not to assert any Patent Claim against any
person (the "User") using the Software or the Data, provided
that:

(a) the Patent Claim reads on functionality of the Software or
    on the structure / compilation of the Data as distributed
    by Licensor;
(b) the User's use is consistent with the applicable licence
    (Apache-2.0 for Software; CC BY 4.0 for Data - see
    [LICENSE](./LICENSE), [LICENSE-CODE.txt](./LICENSE-CODE.txt),
    [LICENSE-DATA.txt](./LICENSE-DATA.txt), and
    [NOTICE](./NOTICE)).

## 3. Relationship to Apache-2.0 §3

For the Software distributed under Apache License 2.0, this
policy is additional to - not a replacement for - the express
patent licence in Apache-2.0 §3. The Apache patent grant
remains the primary grant; this policy restates and extends the
commitment to cover use of the Data as well.

## 4. EU Sui Generis Database Right

To the extent Licensor's sui generis database right over the
compiled Data (Directive 96/9/EC; §§ 87a-87e UrhG) is treated
as equivalent to a patent-like exclusive right under any
jurisdiction's law, the Non-Assertion Commitment in Section 2
applies to that right as well, subject to the same conditions.
The CC BY 4.0 grant in clause 2(a)(1)(B) of that licence
already licences the sui generis right to Users; this policy
makes that position defensively explicit.

## 5. Termination for Patent Aggression

The commitment in Section 2 terminates automatically and
immediately, with respect to the aggressor and any entity under
common control with the aggressor, if that person initiates
patent litigation (including cross-claims and counter-claims)
against Licensor, against the Software or Data, or against any
contributor alleging infringement of a patent.

This mirrors the termination clause in Apache License 2.0 §3.

## 6. Third-Party Patents

Patents held by third parties in respect of components invoked
by the Software (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Mistral, Groq,
DeepSeek for the LLM pipelines; Qdrant for the vector database;
the n8n runtime; individual Python/R/Rust/JavaScript
dependencies) are governed by each third party's licence and
terms. This policy does not grant any licence under those
third-party patents.

## 7. No Waiver of Trademark Rights

This policy covers patents and the sui generis database right
only. It does not licence any trademark. See
[TRADEMARK.md](./TRADEMARK.md) for the trademark policy.

## 8. Future Assignment

If Licensor transfers ownership of this project to a successor
legal entity - including a German Unternehmergesellschaft
(haftungsbeschränkt) (UG) or a Gesellschaft mit beschränkter
Haftung (GmbH) formed by Artem Boiko - the successor is bound
by this policy on the same terms as a condition of the transfer.
